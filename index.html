The 6 Weeks

What you'll move through

Each week runs on the same engine — The Life That Fits Inquiry™ — applied to a different layer of your life. You don't just learn. You shift.

Week 01

The Wrong Pond

Stop the self-interrogation. Map the relationships, roles, and rooms that ask you to be smaller — and start telling the truth about which ones let you breathe.

Week 02

The Unseen Wound

Find the sentence running underneath everything. The quiet conclusion you drew about yourself — and drag it into the light where it can finally be questioned.

Week 03

The Good Daughter

Separate love from self-abandonment. Understand why guilt isn't a moral compass — and start untangling the role you've been playing from who you actually are.

Week 04

The Right Pond

Stop waiting for permission. Learn what you actually need to thrive — and begin building conditions that support the real you, not the performing you.

Week 05

The New Story

You've seen the old belief. Now you build the new one — not as a mantra, but as a lived identity. Who you are when you're not running on fear.

Week 06

A Life That Fits

Bring it all together. Design the life that fits — your work, your relationships, your daily rhythm — and leave with a clear map of what comes next.
